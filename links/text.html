Modern digital electronic computers can perform generic sets of operations known as programs
. These programs enable computers to perform a wide range of tasks.
The term computer system may refer to a nominally complete computer that includes the hardware,
operating system, software, and peripheral equipment needed and used for full operation;
or to a group of computers that are linked and function together, such as a computer network or computer cluster.
